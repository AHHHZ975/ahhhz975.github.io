Biography

I'm Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Mila - Quebec AI Institute and Concordia University under the supervision of Professors Eugene Belilovsky, Tiberiu Popa, and Amir Aghdam. My research focuses on 3D computer vision and computer graphics. I am also working on problems at the intersection of deep reinforcement learning and robotics.

Prior to joining Mila, I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Amirkabir University, Tehran, Iran and my M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Concordia University, Montreal, Canada.

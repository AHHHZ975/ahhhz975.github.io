Biography

I'm Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Mila - Quebec AI Institute and Concordia University under the supervision of Professors Eugene Belilovsky, Tiberiu Popa, and Amir Aghdam. My research focuses primarily on current challenges in computer graphics by combining machine learning with numerical methods for the M&E space.

Prior to joining Autodesk, I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng., both in Computer Engineering at the École de technologie supérieure (ÉTS), supervised by Eric Paquette, and my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Université de Montréal, advised by Pierre Poulin, where I explored ways to improve numerical simulations for particle-based fluids by leveraging hybrid and data-driven methods using iterative solvers.
